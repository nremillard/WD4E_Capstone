Limbs

Contrary to popular belief, Octopuses do not have 8 tentacles and actually have 6 arms and 2 legs.

Octopus (Photo: Pixabay)

Reuse Plastic

The word Octopus comes from Greek origins so the correct plural is Octopuses and not Octopi.

Blue Ring Octopus (Photo: Pixabay)

Blood

Octopuses have blue blood because their blood contains hemocyanin, a copper-based protein that appears blue.

Glass Octopus (Photo: Pixabay)

Intellegence

Octopuses are considered to be the most intelligent of all invertebrates. They can use tools, open jars, complete mazes, and even recognize individual human faces.

Swimming Octopus (Photo: Pixabay)

Hearts

Octopuses have three hearts. Two hearts pump blood to the gills, and a third heart keeps circulation flowing to the organs.
